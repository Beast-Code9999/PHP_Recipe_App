Sunset Risotto

Ingredients

  • 1 cup arborio rice
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  • 1 tbsp butter

Instructions

  1. Saut√© onion in butter until soft.
  2. Add rice and stir for 1 minute.
  3. Gradually add broth, stirring constantly.
  4. When creamy, stir in parmesan.
