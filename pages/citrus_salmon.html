Citrus Salmon

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 orange (juiced)
  • 1 lemon (juiced)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix citrus juices and olive oil.
  3. Place salmon in a baking dish, pour over citrus mix.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes.
